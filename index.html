Learn About Lifting the Corporate Veil

When a company is used for fraud or to dodge legal duties, courts may 'lift the corporate veil' to hold owners personally liable. For example, if a company hides debts, its owners might have to pay. LawAid explains complex terms simply and connects you to corporate law experts.
